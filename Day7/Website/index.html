Turmeric

Turmeric's benefits include powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties due to its active compound, curcumin. It supports joint health, aids digestion, and may contribute to cardiovascular and brain health. Additionally, turmeric has been traditionally used to promote skin health and support immune function.

Yarsagumba

Yarsagumba, a natural hybrid of fungus and caterpillar found in the Himalayas, offers advanced health benefits.Known for enhancing libido and sexual performance. Improves physical endurance and vitality. Strengthens immune defenses against infections. Helps combat oxidative stress and promotes overall well-being.

Timur

Timur, also known as Sichuan pepper, provides a range of advanced health benefits.Alleviates indigestion and promotes gut health.Reduces inflammation and associated discomfort. Fights against bacterial and fungal infections. Protects cells from oxidative damage, supporting overall health.
